Rukhsar's story

Rukhsar learned chikankari at age 11 from her grandmother in the lanes of Aminabad. For nearly two decades she supplied her work to local dalals, earning ₹300–₹400 for pieces that sold for ₹2,000 in Hazratganj. Since joining CHIKANcury, she earns 85% of every sale — directly, within 24 hours.

Her speciality is the Murri stitch, a raised peppercorn-like knot that requires extreme precision. She can work 300+ Murri stitches per hour — a pace few karigars match.
